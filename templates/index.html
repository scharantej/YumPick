Dish of the Week

Welcome to the Dish of the Week website! Here, you can vote for your favorite dish, see the current dish of the week, and order it online.

The dish of the week is chosen by popular vote. Every week, we put up a poll of dishes, and the dish with the most votes becomes the dish of the week.
